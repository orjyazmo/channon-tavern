About The Channon Tavern

The Channon Tavern has re-opened under new management who are focused on creating a family friendly, fun and community-minded space for locals and visitors to enjoy.

Perched by Terania Creek, The Channon Tavern has loads of great seating outside in our spacious picnic area for when the sun is shining, and cosy couches by the fireplace inside for when it gets chilly out here in the hills.

Our restaurant, The Channon Shokudou, is serving up amazing quality sushi and Japanese meals for great prices, and will be including pub classics on the menu ASAP.

We have a jungle gym for the youngins, complete with a cage to keep them trapped inside!

Our pool tables are free to use.

Dogs are welcome outside on leads.

We no longer have Keno or Pokies - apologies to our potential customers that see this as a negative.

The owners and management are intent on improving The Channon Tavern bit by bit. For all the great feedback we've had from the community and visitors alike, we still consider The Tavern - and this website for that matter - to be a work in progress. But what glorious work it is!

We update our Instagram and Facebook more frequently than this website, please follow us to keep up with what's on and see all round wholesome imagery of The Tavern.
