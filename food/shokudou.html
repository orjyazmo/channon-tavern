The Channon Shokudou is The Channon Tavern's very own Japanese restaurant.

The chefs from The Channon Shokudou ("Shokudou" meaning "Food Hall" in Japanese) have already been blessing us with great quality sushi and knee-slappingly good hot food for the last little while, and soon they'll be adding some pub classics and winter warmers to the menu for all to enjoy!
