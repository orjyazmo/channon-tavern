The Channon Shokudou is The Channon Tavern's very own Japanese restaurant.

The chefs from The Channon Shokudou ("Shokudou" meaning "Food Hall" in Japanese) have been blessing us with more than just great quality sushi - there's pub classics and tasty side meals on offer too. All at great prices!
